About Us

Welcome to Exodia, an innovative web application designed to assist penetration testers with AI-powered tools. Our platform leverages large language models (LLMs) to help security professionals in identifying vulnerabilities, crafting detailed reports, and generating exploit code.

What is Exodia?

Exodia is an advanced web application designed to assist penetration testers with AI-powered tools. It helps in identifying vulnerabilities, generating detailed reports, and creating exploit codes, making the penetration testing process more efficient and effective.

Our Models

We use two types of models: a fast, less accurate model and a more accurate, slower model. The fast model is ideal for quick tasks, while the accurate model is used for detailed analysis and precision.

Our Team

We are a team of four students from the Faculty of Science at Zagazig University, united by our passion for cybersecurity and technology. We are dedicated to enhancing the field of penetration testing through innovative solutions.

Technology Used

  • Django - Web framework for backend development
  • Tailwind CSS - Utility-first CSS framework for styling
  • Large Language Models - AI models for enhanced functionality
  • Python - Programming language for implementing AI and backend logic

Our Vision

We envision a future where penetration testing is significantly enhanced by AI, making security assessments faster, more accurate, and more insightful.
